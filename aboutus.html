ABOUT US

BACKGROUND

TECHFEST 2025 is a national-scale technology festival organized by HIMTI BINUS University, designed to ignite creativity, enhance tech literacy, and foster collaboration among students from universities across Indonesia. This year, under the theme MAGIC — Mastering Analytics & Graphics in Innovation Competitions, we highlight the fusion between data and design in creating impactful solutions.

HISTORY

TECHFEST first started in 2021 as “HIMTI Festival,” held online from Malang. Even during the pandemic, students actively engaged and kept the tech spirit alive. In 2025, TECHFEST enters its 5th year with even greater ambition.

GOALS

To empower students through real-case competitions, insightful seminars, and collaborative innovation, TECHFEST 2025 aims to develop a generation of tech-savvy, future-ready individuals.
