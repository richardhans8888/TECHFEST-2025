Technology Festival (TECHFEST) 2025 is a series of national scale events held by HIMTI annually. It is designed to foster creativity and competition among students across Indonesia.

The theme of this year’s TECHFEST is MAGIC: Mastering Analytics & Graphics in Innovation Competitions.

TECHFEST 2025 consists of three main activities, namely competitions, showcase, and seminars (TechTalk).

TECHFEST 2025 activities will be held as follows: competitions at BINUS University @Kemanggisan - Anggrek Campus, showcase online on this website, and TechTalk both onsite at BINUS University @Kemanggisan - Anggrek Campus and online via Zoom.

TECHFEST 2025 will host two competitions, namely UI/UX Design and Data Analytics

Registration can be done through the TechTalk and Competition page

Yes, the registration fees are as follows: IDR 100.000 for Data Analytics Competition, and IDR 50.000 for UI/UX Design Competition, and for TechTalk will not be charged

Yes, participants can register for several competitions, but team leaders can only participate in one.

The number of participants in each team are as follows: 2-3 people for data analytics competition and 1-3 people for UI/UX Design.

Team members must come from the same university, but may be from different majors.

Participants of the competitions must be an active student of higher study(S1/D3/D4).
